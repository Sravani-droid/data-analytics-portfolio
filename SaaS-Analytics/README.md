# SaaS Customer Churn & Revenue Retention Dashboard

## Overview
This Tableau dashboard analyzes SaaS customer churn behavior, subscription patterns, and revenue retention metrics to identify drivers of churn and opportunities for customer retention.

## Objective
Help stakeholders understand:
- Which customer segments are most likely to churn
- How churn varies across contract types and payment methods
- Which product/service packages show highest churn risk
- Which customers should be prioritized for retention campaigns

## Dashboard Highlights
- ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)
- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
- Average Customer Tenure
- Overall Churn Rate
- Churn by Contract Type
- Churn by Payment Method
- Churn by Product/Service Package
- Churn by Tenure Bucket
- Revenue by Contract
- At Risk Customer List

## Key Insights
- Month-to-month contracts exhibit significantly higher churn than long-term contracts
- Electronic check customers show the highest churn rates among payment methods
- Fiber optic customers churn at a higher rate than DSL or no internet service customers
- Customers in their first 12 months have the highest churn probability
