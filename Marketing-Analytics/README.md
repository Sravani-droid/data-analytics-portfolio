# Marketing Campaign ROI & Customer Response Dashboard

## Overview
This Tableau dashboard analyzes marketing campaign performance, customer response behavior, purchasing trends, and customer segmentation. It helps businesses understand which customer groups respond best, where revenue comes from, and how to improve campaign ROI.

## Tools Used
- Tableau Public
- Excel / CSV
- Data Cleaning
- Business Intelligence
- Customer Segmentation

## Key KPIs
- Campaign Response Rate: 14.9%
- High Value Customers: 2,240
- Total Customers: 2,240
- Avg Purchases per Customer: 12.5
- Avg Customer Spend: $605.8

## Dashboard Insights

### Customer Response
- Customers aged 30–44 show the highest campaign response rate.
- Recent customers respond more often than inactive customers.
- Previous responders convert significantly better than new prospects.

### Revenue & Spend
- High-value customers generate the largest share of total spend.
- Wines and meat products drive the highest product-category revenue.

### Channel Performance
- Catalog customers have the highest average purchases.
- Web traffic does not always convert into campaign responses.

## Business Recommendations
- Retarget previous responders first.
- Focus campaigns on high-value customer segments.
- Re-engage recent customers before churn risk increases.
- Improve website conversion funnel.
- Increase investment in top-performing channels.
