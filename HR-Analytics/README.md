# Employee Attrition & Workforce Risk Dashboard

## Overview

This Tableau dashboard analyzes employee attrition patterns to identify workforce risk indicators across departments, salary bands, overtime groups, job satisfaction, and employee demographics.

## Objective

Help stakeholders understand:

* Which departments face the highest attrition risk
* How overtime impacts employee turnover
* Which salary bands have elevated attrition
* Which age groups are more likely to leave
* How job satisfaction correlates with retention

## Key Insights

* Sales department shows the highest attrition rate
* Employees working overtime have significantly higher attrition
* Low salary bands exhibit the greatest turnover risk
* Younger employees have elevated attrition rates
* Lower job satisfaction correlates with increased attrition

## Tools Used

* Tableau Public
* Data Visualization
* Workforce Analytics
