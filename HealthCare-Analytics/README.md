# 🏥 Hospital Readmission Risk & Diabetes Care Dashboard

An interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing hospital readmissions, patient risk factors, and diabetes care indicators to identify key drivers of readmission and opportunities to reduce healthcare costs.


## 📊 Dashboard Overview

This dashboard focuses on understanding **why patients are readmitted** and identifying **high-risk patient groups**, especially in diabetes care.


## 🔍 Key Business Questions

- Which patient groups have the highest readmission risk?
- How does **length of stay** impact readmission rates?
- Do **prior hospital visits** increase readmission probability?
- How do **A1C test results** relate to readmission risk?
- Which diagnoses contribute most to readmissions?

## 📈 Key Insights

- 🔴 **Diabetes patients show the highest readmission risk** among all diagnosis groups.
- 📈 **Readmission risk increases significantly with prior inpatient visits.**
- ⏳ **Patients with longer hospital stays (8+ days)** have the highest readmission rates.
- ⚠️ **Poor A1C results are associated with higher readmission risk**, indicating gaps in diabetes management.
- 👥 Older age groups show slightly higher readmission trends.


## 💡 Business Impact

- Hospitals can **reduce costs** by targeting high-risk patients early.
- Focus on **diabetes care management** to reduce repeat admissions.
- Improve **post-discharge monitoring** for patients with long stays.
- Use **prior visit history** to proactively flag high-risk patients.
